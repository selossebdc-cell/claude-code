## What's Delivered (Standard Tier — €149)

### 1. **Diagnostic Interview** (Live or Async)
- **Duration**: 30–45 minutes
- **Format**: Intelligent conversation (9 implicit blocks, adaptive questioning)
- **Output**: Synthesis document (see below)
- **Outcome Measured**: Client understands their gaps + sees specific Claude opportunities

### 2. **Strategic Synthesis** (Written Document)
Generated post-diagnosis. Structure:

```
# Ce que j'ai compris de vous
- Your consulting role + key context
- 3–5 main pain points (specific, grounded)
- Your work style + strengths
- Where Claude transforms (why it's different)

# Où Claude devient vraiment game-changer
For each of 3–5 opportunities:
- What it solves (specific pain point)
- Why Claude transforms it (not just "can help")
- Which agent handles it
- Expected impact (time saved, clarity gained, decisions accelerated)

# Votre config sera centrée sur
- 6 Core Agents (with roles + daily trigger)
- "Ma Mémoire" project (personal knowledge hub)
- Custom Instructions (2000+ chars, hyper-specific)
- Weekly routines (3–5 recommended, with timing)
- Implementation roadmap (week 1, 2, month 1)
```

**Outcome Measured**: Client sees clear "before/after" for their situation

---

### 3. **6 Core Agents — Pre-Configured**

#### **Miroir** (The Mirror)
- **Role**: Daily reflection on priorities, patterns, decisions
- **Trigger**: Every morning (9 AM by default)
- **Output**: 5-min personalized brief
- **Customization**: Client's specific role + KPIs injected into prompt
- **Outcome**: "I understand my day's purpose"

#### **Garde-Fou** (The Guard Rail)
- **Role**: Decision validation + risk flagging
- **Trigger**: On-demand when making important decisions
- **Output**: Decision scorecard (risk levels, missing info, alternatives)
- **Customization**: Client's decision type (hiring, strategy, budget) + risk appetite
- **Outcome**: "I make fewer mistakes"

#### **Admin** (The Operations Brain)
- **Role**: Process optimization + checklists
- **Trigger**: Weekly (Mondays 10 AM) or on-demand
- **Output**: Runbook + bottleneck alerts
- **Customization**: Client's 3 main processes (scheduling, reporting, etc.)
- **Outcome**: "My team operates on autopilot"

#### **Stratégie** (The Strategist)
- **Role**: Quarterly planning + scenario modeling
- **Trigger**: Monthly strategy deep-dive
- **Output**: Strategy document + 3 scenarios
- **Customization**: Client's business model + growth goals
- **Outcome**: "I have a clear 3-month roadmap"

#### **Planification** (The Planner)
- **Role**: Task breakdown + dependency mapping
- **Trigger**: When faced with big projects
- **Output**: Gantt-like breakdown + resource allocation
- **Customization**: Client's team size + complexity tolerance
- **Outcome**: "Large projects don't feel overwhelming"

#### **Amélioration Continue** (The Improvement Agent)
- **Role**: Weekly retrospective + process refinement
- **Trigger**: Friday 4 PM (reflection time)
- **Output**: Improvement log + next week's experiments
- **Customization**: Client's learning style + business KPIs
- **Outcome**: "I improve 1% every week, compounding"

### 4. **"Ma Mémoire" Project** (Personal Knowledge Hub)

**What It Is**:
- Personalized Notion/Claude-native workspace
- Stores: Client's context, past decisions, patterns, learnings, proprietary knowledge

**Structure**:
```
Ma Mémoire/
├── Identity (role, 5-year vision, values)
├── Business Context (market, competitors, team, financials)
├── Decision Log (decisions made + why, outcomes tracked)
├── Patterns (what works, what doesn't)
├── Relationships Map (key clients/stakeholders, how to handle)
├── Knowledge Base (proprietary frameworks, templates, playbooks)
└── Weekly Learnings (what Claude taught you this week)
```

**Auto-Populated With**:
- Diagnostic insights (from synthesis)
- Agent outputs (automatically summarized + stored)
- Client inputs (manual entries)

**Outcome Measured**: "Claude remembers my context. Responses get better over time."

---

### 5. **Custom Instructions** (Hyper-Specific)

**Length**: 2000–3000 characters (substantial, not generic)

**Covers**:
- Client's role + decision-making style
- Constraints (budget, team size, risk tolerance)
- Tone + communication preference
- Examples of good work (for pattern matching)
- Blind spots to watch for
- What success looks like

**Example** (Consultant):
```
You are advising a management consultant in France with 8 years experience 
building strategy for mid-market B2B SaaS (€2–50M ARR).

Decision-making style: Data-driven + intuition, values speed over perfection.
Constraints: 20 hours/week for consulting work, solo operator.
What I need: Clear hypotheses + test plans, not lengthy analysis.

Blind spot: Tendency to over-engineer solutions. Push back with "what's the MVP?"
Success: When I reduce a complex problem to 3 options + 1 recommendation in <1hr.
```

**Outcome Measured**: "Claude understands my context and my constraints."

---

### 6. **Weekly Routines** (3–5 Recommended)

Pre-built routines with timing + triggers:

**Monday Morning** (15 min)
- Agent: Miroir + Stratégie
- Prompt: "What are my top 3 priorities this week?"
- Output: Weekly focus document

**Wednesday Midday** (20 min)
- Agent: Garde-Fou
- Prompt: "Review my decisions from this week so far. What's risky?"
- Output: Decision audit + risk flags

**Friday Evening** (30 min)
- Agent: Amélioration Continue
- Prompt: "What worked this week? What failed? What's the experiment for next week?"
- Output: Weekly retrospective + learning capture

**Monthly** (1 hour, first Monday)
- Agent: Stratégie
- Prompt: "Are we on track for quarterly goals? What needs to shift?"
- Output: Monthly review + adjustments

**As-Needed**
- Agent: Admin, Planification
- Triggers: "When I have a project >5 days of work" or "When ops feels chaotic"

### 7. **Implementation Roadmap** (Phased)

**Week 1: Setup**
- Agents activated + routed to client's default tools (Claude.com, API, etc.)
- Ma Mémoire seeded with diagnostic insights
- First routine scheduled (Monday Miroir)

**Week 2: Integration**
- Client uses 3 agents in live decisions (Miroir, Garde-Fou, Stratégie)
- Feedback loop: what's working, what needs tuning

**Week 3–4: Expansion**
- All 6 agents active + routines running
- Custom Instructions refined based on usage patterns
- Ma Mémoire manually enriched by client

**Month 2+: Optimization**
- Agents tuned based on 30 days of usage
- New routines added (experiment phase)
- Ma Mémoire becomes client's competitive advantage
